##Dictionary Merge and Update Operators
-->New operators | and  |= allow for merging and updating dictionaries.

dict1 = {'a': 1 , 'b': 2}
dict2= {'b': 3 , 'c': 4}
merged = dict1 | dict2
print(merged) #output: {'a': 1 , 'b':3 , 'c': 4}
